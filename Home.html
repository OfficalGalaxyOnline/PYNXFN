What is MythFN?
MythFN is a Fortnite private server that will change the world of fortnite private servers forever. This project is made to give you and our community the best experience while hanging with friends and or with random strangers that are also using MythFN. We make sure that everyone has a fair advantage and let's the user go back to ANY season (e.g., Chapter 1 Season 1, Etc). 

What features are coming to MythFN?

MythFN will have alot of fun and exciting features like in-game support, Skins, Custom Lobby Backgrounds, and more! we will make sure you get everything that you wish for!
